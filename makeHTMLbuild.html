Build an AR System

Designing an AR system from scratch is immensely complex, even more complicated than it would be to build a cellphone from scratch. Many of the components involved are not commercially available and would have to be custom engineered. Even just an AR display by itself would be an almost insurmountable challenge to create. Many genius with PhD’s in optics have tried and failed to invent a compact near-eye volumetric display.

It may be difficult, but I don’t think it’s impossible to build a compelling AR system. This page discusses some of the components and variables necessary to consider for AR. It is in no way exhaustive: for example it doesn’t even begin to cover input methods. However, if you are interested in building an AR system, I hope this page gives you an idea of what factors need to be considered.
