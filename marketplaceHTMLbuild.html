AR You Can Buy

There are three augmented reality systems in development that should be available to consumers sometime in 2016. Many tech companies such as Apple and Sony have recently hired or created teams to work on AR, but it is likely that these products will be further out. In addition, countless tiny startups are working on Google Glass-style devices or enterprise systems, but I have decided that none of them appear promising enough to be included here.
